Cinnamon Roll Recipe

An Easy to make recipe for desert.


Ingredients For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.

  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.

  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.

  4. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.

  5. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.

  6. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.

And thats it!! Enjoy your cinnamon rolls with tea and snacks.
